# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

---

## Scrum Master

### Role Summary
The Scrum Master is responsible for facilitating Agile ceremonies, coaching the team on process improvements, and ensuring best practices in delivery.

### Responsibilities
- Facilitate Agile ceremonies (standups, planning, retrospectives, demos)
- Act as a process coach, helping the team continuously improve
- Remove blockers impacting the team's progress
- Coordinate closely with PM and PdM to keep delivery on track
- Ensure scrum practices are followed and adapted as needed

### Goals
- Enable teams to deliver value consistently
- Improve team efficiency and morale
- Foster a culture of continuous improvement

### Typical Communication
- Daily with the delivery team
- Regular check-ins with PM and PdM on progress/blockers
- Facilitates retrospectives and team alignment sessions

---

## UX Designer

### Role Summary
UX Designers focus on optimizing the overall user experience by creating intuitive journeys, wireframes, and prototypes, ensuring solutions meet user needs.

### Responsibilities
- Craft user journeys, wireframes, and prototypes
- Conduct user research and usability testing
- Collaborate with Product Managers to translate user needs into requirements and user stories
- Work closely with Developers to clarify requirements and hand off designs

### Goals
- Deliver usable, accessible, and delightful product experiences
- Reduce UX debt through systematic testing and iteration
- Ensure user needs are represented in the backlog

### Typical Communication
- Works closely with Product Managers and Developers
- Participates in discovery sessions and design reviews
- Shares insights from user testing and research

---

## Business Analyst

### Role Summary
The Business Analyst gathers and documents requirements, maps business processes, and acts as a bridge between stakeholders and the delivery team.

### Responsibilities
- Gather and document detailed requirements from stakeholders
- Map out existing and future business processes
- Prepare user stories and define acceptance criteria
- Support PM/PdM in prioritization and backlog refinement

### Goals
- Ensure business and technical alignment on priorities
- Identify process improvements and potential issues early
- Translate business goals into actionable work

### Typical Communication
- Meetings with stakeholders and delivery team
- Documentation of business needs, flows, and requirements
- Regular updates to the PM/PdM

---

## DevOps Engineer

### Role Summary
DevOps Engineers ensure the reliability, monitoring, and automation of software delivery, supporting the entire team through robust CICD and deployment processes.

### Responsibilities
- Manage CI/CD pipelines and deployment automation
- Implement and monitor system metrics and alerts
- Collaborate with Developers and QA to streamline release cycles
- Support troubleshooting, rollback, and incident response

### Goals
- Accelerate and stabilize software delivery cycles
- Ensure system uptime and rapid recovery
- Automate repeatable operational tasks

### Typical Communication
- Involved in planning and release coordination
- Works closely with Developers and QA during releases
- Notification and troubleshooting during incidents
